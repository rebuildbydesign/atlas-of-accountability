How To Use

1) Explore The Map: Navigate the interactive map to view counties impacted by extreme weather or use the search function to find a specific address.

2) County-Level Information: Click on a county to see the number of disaster declarations and detailed information about your congressional and state representatives.

3) Take Action: Use the provided data to contact your representatives and advocate for stronger, resilient infrastructure in your community.

Methodology

This map is built on a foundation of research and data sources provided by U.S. Government:

  • FEMA Data: Disaster declarations for climate events occurring 2011-2023, courtesy iParametrics
  • County Districts: Geographic boundaries from ESRI (2020)
  • Congressional Districts: Geographic boundaries from USDOT BTS
  • Population: U.S. Census ACS 2018-2022 (5-Year Estimate)
